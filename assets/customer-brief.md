# Company Background: Mölnlycke Health Care

## About Your Company

**Mölnlycke Health Care** is a world-leading MedTech company headquartered in Gothenburg, Sweden, specialising in innovative solutions for wound care and surgical procedures. Founded in 1849 and owned by Investor AB (Patricia Industries), Mölnlycke operates in over 100 countries with four business areas: Wound Care, OR Solutions, Gloves, and Antiseptics. With annual sales of EUR 2,064 million and 7.4% year-over-year growth, the company is one of the top five global players in advanced wound care.

## Pain Points Identified by Leadership

### Strategic Challenges
1. **Foam dressing battle intensifying:** Smith+Nephew ALLEVYN and Coloplast Biatain gaining share vs Mepilex in key EU markets
2. **NPWT market fragmentation:** Avance Solo competes against Smith+Nephew PICO and Solventum V.A.C. with unclear differentiation messaging
3. **Procurement-driven decisions:** Hospital GPOs increasingly favour lowest-cost options, eroding Mölnlycke's premium positioning
4. **Category gaps:** Fast-growing segments like bioactive wound care and digital wound assessment lack a strong Mölnlycke entry
5. **Sustainability pressure:** EU hospitals adding green procurement criteria; competitors positioning sustainability as a differentiator

### Operational Challenges
1. **Manual competitive tracking:** Market intelligence team spends 20+ hours/month monitoring competitor product feedback across clinical forums, MedTech publications, and procurement platforms
2. **Delayed clinical feedback:** Product complaints and praise from clinicians take 3-6 months to reach R&D teams
3. **No cross-business-area view:** Wound Care, OR Solutions, Gloves, and Antiseptics each track market data in separate systems
4. **Clinical evidence blind spots:** We don't know which product features drive clinician preference for competitor products
